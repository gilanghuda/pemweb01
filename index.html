Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and various payment methods depending on your country.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time. Your Premium benefits will continue until the next billing date.

What is included in Premium?

Premium includes ad-free music listening, the ability to download songs for offline listening, on-demand playback, and higher quality audio.

How do Family and Duo plans work?

Family plans allow up to 6 people living at the same address to share one Premium subscription. Duo plans are for 2 people living at the same address.
